About Us

Our Mission & Story

Founded on a simple belief: every person deserves skilled, compassionate care in the comfort and dignity of their own home.

Who We Are

Rooted in Community

Global Home Health Care LLC was founded in Columbus, Ohio with a simple but powerful vision: to deliver world-class medical care to patients where they are most comfortable — at home, surrounded by family.

Since 2021, we have proudly served families across the Columbus metropolitan area, building lasting relationships based on trust, transparency, and clinical excellence. Our team of licensed nurses, certified aides, and licensed therapists brings not only professional expertise but genuine compassion to every patient interaction.

We understand that choosing a home health provider is one of the most important decisions a family makes. That's why we hold ourselves to the highest standards — in our hiring, our training, and our ongoing commitment to quality care.
