FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept insurance?
Yes! We accept Medicare, Medicaid, and most private insurance plans. Contact us to verify your coverage before starting services.
Is care available on weekends?
Absolutely. We offer care 7 days a week, including weekends and holidays. Your loved one's needs don't take a day off, and neither do we.
Can I change caregivers if needed?
Your comfort and satisfaction are our top priority. If a caregiver isn't the right fit, we'll work with you to find someone who is.
How do I refer a patient?
Physicians, discharge planners, and family members can submit referrals online through our referral page, or call us directly at (614) 495-8934.
Are your caregivers background-checked?
Yes. All staff undergo comprehensive background checks, reference verifications, drug testing, and skills assessments before joining our team.
Do you offer multilingual care?
Yes. We have multilingual caregivers available upon request to serve the diverse communities of Columbus and surrounding areas.
